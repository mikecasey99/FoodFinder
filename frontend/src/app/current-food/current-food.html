Stuffed Bell Peppers with Quinoa and Black Beans

Category: Vegetarian
Area: Mexican

Ingredients

  • 4 whole Green Pepper
  • 1 tablespoon Olive Oil
  • 1 small finely diced Onion
  • 2 cloves minced Garlic
  • 1 cup Quinoa
  • 1 can Black Beans
  • 1 cup Sweetcorn
  • 1 can Diced Tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon Cumin
  • ½ tsp Chili Powder
  • ½ tsp Smoked Paprika
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Pepper (to taste)
  • 1 1/2 cup Shredded Mexican Cheese
  • Chopped Cilantro

Instructions

1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C)...
2. Bake bell pepper halves cut-side-up for 15-20 minutes...
3. Sauté onion and garlic, add other ingredients...
4. Stuff the peppers, top with cheese, and bake again...
5. Garnish with cilantro and serve warm.
